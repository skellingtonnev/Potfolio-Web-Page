About Me

Hello! I'm Tom, a student in my final year at Monash University studying communication design with a specialization in UI/UX!

I've always found great pleasure in tinkering with unique aesthetics, whether in the context of building my own Neocities website in highschool or consuming media with flashy experimental effects.

I chose to pursue UI/UX design because it allows me to create a product that is not only visually appealing, but requires careful consideration of what features would best suit the user.

I enjoy seeing how both of these aspects work in conjunction to result in a product I can take pride in, whether in a team or individual setting.
